Summer Youth Camp 2025

From April 29 to May 1, 2025, young people gathered in Tangub City for a transformative Christian Summer Youth Camp dedicated to cultivating Godly leaders. This intensive program equipped participants with essential leadership skills, all firmly anchored in biblical principles and Christ-like character. Through engaging sessions and activities, youth learned to lead with integrity, humility, and a heart for service, inspired by faith. The camp aimed to empower them to become positive, spiritually-grounded influencers in their churches and communities.

Asia Pacific Youth Conference 2023

This pivotal Asia Pacific Youth Conference brought together young leaders across two focused clusters: October 31-November 1 and November 2-November 3. Designed to empower the next generation, the conference offered a dynamic platform for growth, collaboration, and spiritual formation. Participants engaged in impactful sessions, networking opportunities, and discussions aimed at equipping them to address regional challenges and foster positive change, all while strengthening their shared vision for a vibrant future.
